Why we are here

In my 15 years at ASOS I grew to love what we had built; a one-stop, easy to use fashion shop, selling a trusted edit of the worlds best brands and our own-label.

So, when I became a dad I looked around for a similar trusted and stylish edit of ‘all things kids', but it just wasn't there. Rather than settle for the status quo, I decided to do something about it and, in May 2016, we launched KIDLY, the only store a modern parent needs for their 0-4s.
